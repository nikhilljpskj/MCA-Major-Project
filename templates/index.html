Features

Title feature

The number of title word in sentences, words in sentences that also occur in title gives high score.

Sentence length

The number of word in sentence this feature is useful to filtering out short sentences such as datelines and author names commonly found in news articles

Word embedding

Word embedding is a set of feature learning techniques in NLP where words are mapped to vectors of real numbers

TF - IDF

Determines the relevance of a word in the document

Text Extraction

Extracting keywords or key sentences from the original document without changing the sentences to make the summary

Proper Noun

The number of proper noun in sentence. Usually the sentence contains more proper nouns is important and is included in the document summary
